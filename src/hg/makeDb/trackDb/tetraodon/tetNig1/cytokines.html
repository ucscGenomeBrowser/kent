Description

This track shows cytokine gene annotations which were annotated by human experts using information on sequence similarity between Tetraodon and human, mouse and zebrafish cytokine protein and nucleotide sequences. Cytokine gene annotations are only on the following chromosomes: 1, 2, 3, 4, 7, 8, 10, 12, 13, 15, 16, 1_random, 3_random, and Un_random.
