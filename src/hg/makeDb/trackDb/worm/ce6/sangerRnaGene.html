Description

The WormBase Genes track shows RNA predictions from the Wormbase v. WS190 files downloaded from the Sanger Institute FTP site. This track shows the subset of data annotated as "miRNA", "ncRNA", "rRNA", "scRNA", "snRNA" ,"snlRNA" ,"snoRNA" ,"tRNA", or "tRNAscan-SE-1.23" in the gff files.
