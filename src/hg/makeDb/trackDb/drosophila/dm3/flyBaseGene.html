Description

This track shows protein-coding genes annotated by FlyBase and the Drosophila Heterochromatin Genome Project (DHGP). Annotations on both heterochromatin and euchromatic sequences were downloaded from FlyBase D. melanogaster version 5.12.

Credits

Thanks to FlyBase and DHGP for providing these annotations.
