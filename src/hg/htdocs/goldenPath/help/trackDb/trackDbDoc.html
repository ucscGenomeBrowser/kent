canPack <off/on> all NOT FOR HUBS. Deprecated.

Almost tracks can be displayed in the five standard visibilities. However on some track types such as wiggles, squish and pack offer no real advantage over dense and full. By default, these tracks will not offer the less valuable visibilities. Nevertheless, you can make your track offer these visibilility choices by turning canPack on. Note: subtracks of composites will always offer all five choices.

Example:

   canPack on
