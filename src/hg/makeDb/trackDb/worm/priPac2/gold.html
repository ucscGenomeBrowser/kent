Description

This track shows the draft assembly of the $organism genome.

See also: ABKE0 WGS information.

This genome has been sequenced to 8.92X coverage. There are 13,962 contigs comprising 133,635,077 bases (304 undetermined bases marked as N nucleotides in the sequence). The largest contig, ABKE01000001 is 156,897 bases. One half of the sequence, 66,817,538 bases, is contained in 2135 contigs, all of at least 18,623 bases (N50).

In dense mode, this track depicts the contigs. Contig boundaries are distinguished by the use of alternating gold and brown coloration.

All components within this track are of fragment type "D": Whole Genome Shotgun draft contig.
