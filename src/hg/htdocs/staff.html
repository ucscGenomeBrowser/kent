UCSC Genome Browser Project Team
  UCSC Genome Browser Project Staff:
  • Jim Kent: Research Scientist, co-director of UCSC Genome Browser project. Design and implementation of the Genome Browser and the BLAT sequence search tool. Author of the the UCSC Known Genes annotations, the Gene Sorter, VisiGene, Genome Graphs, chaining and netting programs, the In Silico PCR tool, and the GigAssembler. Implementation of many Genome Browser annotation tracks, including FPC contigs, assembly, gap, coverage, known genes, full mRNA, spliced ESTs, human ESTs, Genscan genes, non-human mRNA and EST, and mouse BLAT.
  • Galt Barber: Genome Browser engineering, Cow Browser, VisiGene.
