Description

This track shows epitopes that were validated in published studies, mostly from the SARS 2003 outbreak.

Summary

The epitope sequences were collected by Nerli et al, partially from the IEDB database, partially from the original literature. The details page shows how similar the SARS-Cov-2 epitope is to the SARS epitope.
