UCSC Genome Browser: Site Down for Temporary Maintenance

This UCSC Genome Browser site is currently down for temporary maintenance. Our system administrators are working to bring the site back for public use as soon as possible.

UCSC Genome Browser Alternatives

For alternatives to this UCSC Genome Browser, please use one of the official sites listed below. Please note that any saved sessions and custom tracks from this site may not be available on other sites or mirrors.

  • U.S.: genome.ucsc.edu
  • Europe mirror: genome-euro.ucsc.edu
  • Asia mirror: genome-asia.ucsc.edu

The UCSC Genome Browser collaborates with the following groups to provide mirror sites that may be used during network or power outages on the UCSC campus. Please limit the use of these sites to occasions when the UCSC Genome Browser (U.S., Europe, or Asia) sites are unavailable.

  • genome-mirror.cshl.edu - provided by the Simons Center for Quantitative Biology at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory (CSHL). This mirror may contain additional CSHL-generated annotation in addition to the standard UCSC tracks. Many thanks to Adam Siepel at CSHL for providing this mirror.

Help & Support

To reach the UCSC Genome Browser support team, please email our confidential email list (not publicly displayed) at: genome-www@soe.ucsc.edu. In addition, the UCSC Genome Browser public support forum may contain details about this outage.
