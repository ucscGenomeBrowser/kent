  The following set of UCSC summary statistics differ slightly from those reported by NCBI in these ways:

  • NCBI identified 33 contigs as heterochromatin. The UCSC numbers include the euchromatin
    and heterochromatin contigs combined, and also include the mitochondrial genome as an
    euchromatin contig.
  • UCSC includes the two alternative haplotypes for the MHC region in chr6 in the _random
    section for that chromosome. As a result, they show up in the unlocalized stats. NCBI
    splits out the two alternative haplotypes for this MHC region.
  • After subtracting out the alt haplotypes, UCSC reports 24,799,811 unlocalized bases, which
    differs slightly from NCBI's number. This may be due to a last-minute rearrangement of
    chromosome 8, which UCSC included in its stats but NCBI may not have included.
