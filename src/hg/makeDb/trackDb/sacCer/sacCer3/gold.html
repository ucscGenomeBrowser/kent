Description

This track shows the final assembly of the Saccharomyces cerevisiae S288c assembly (GCA_000146055.2) as of April 2011. of the $organism genome. Please note the sequencing status at: SGD™.

Chromosomes available in this assembly: chrI, chrII, chrIII, chrIV ... etc ... chrXVI, and chrM. See also: SGD™ Genome Snapshot/Overview.
