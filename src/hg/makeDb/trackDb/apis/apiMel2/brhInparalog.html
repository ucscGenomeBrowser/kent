Description

This track shows gene predictions produced by Evgeny Zdobnov at EMBL, based on homology of UniRef50 proteins with the honeybee genomic sequence.

Methods

These gene predictions result from a comparison of the A. mellifera genomic sequence with UniRef50 known proteins using tBlastN. For each genomic region, the protein most similar to the genomic sequence was used for homology-based gene-model prediction with FgenSH+. High confidence orthology relations among candidate predictions in different insect species were identified using all-vs-all Smith-Waterman comparisons.
