Description

The WormBase Genes track shows Sanger Gene predictions from the Wormbase v. WS190 files downloaded from the Sanger Institute FTP site. This track shows the subset of data annotated as "curated", or "Coding_transcript" followed by one of these strings: "intron", "coding_exon", "exon", "cds", "three_prime_UTR", or "five_prime_UTR" in the gff files.
