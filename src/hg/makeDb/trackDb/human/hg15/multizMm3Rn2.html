Description

This track displays multiz alignments of the Feb. 2003 mouse draft assembly and the rat Jan. 2003 assembly to the $organism genome.

Methods

Multiz is a multiple alignment program that takes blastz /axtBest alignments as input. It uses the same scoring matrix as blastz between pairs of sequences: 

      A     C     G     T
A    91  -114   -31  -123
C  -114   100  -125   -31
G   -31  -125   100  -114
T  -123   -31  -114    91

O = 400, E = 30, K = 3000, L = 3000, M = 50

The overall score is the sum of the score between all pairs.
