Description

This is a collection of tracks that annotate individual gene loci or gene families that benefit from a curated, locus-specific representation beyond what the standard gene-prediction tracks provide. The tracks here are typically derived from focused studies that examined a single gene or a small set of related genes in detail.

Subtracks currently in this collection:

  • Mucin VNTR exons — the variable-number tandem repeat exons of the human mucin gene family.
  • Mucin non-VNTR exons — the remaining (non-VNTR) exons of the human mucin gene family.

Data Access

Each subtrack has its own description page with full details on the data source, processing steps, and download instructions.
