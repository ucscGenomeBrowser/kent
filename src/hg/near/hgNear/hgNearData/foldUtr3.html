Description

This column shows the estimated energy in kcal/mol of folding the 3' UTR of a gene into the best predicted secondary structure.

Methods

The energy calculations and secondary structure predictions are from the RNAfold program, which is part of the Vienna RNA Package.

References

Hofacker IL. Vienna RNA secondary structure server. Nucleic Acids Res. 2003 July 1;31(13):3429-31.
