Description

This track shows annotated genes and open reading frames (ORFs) of Saccharomyces cerevisiae obtained from the Saccharomyces Genome Database (SGD). The data were downloaded from the site
http://downloads.yeastgenome.org/chromosomal_feature/saccharomyces_cerevisiae.gff on 29 Aug. 2011. This track excludes the ORFs classified as dubious by SGD. Clicking on an item in this track brings up a display that synthesizes available data on the gene from a wide variety of sources.
