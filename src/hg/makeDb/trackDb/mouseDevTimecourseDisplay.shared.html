Display Conventions and Configuration

Tracks are displayed as bigBarCharts. Bars are colored by tissue and developmental time point, with the shade darkening as the time point advances from e10.5 through P0. Bars can be grouped and filtered by tissue and time point using the track configuration controls.

Bars are colored by tissue, with a gradient that darkens as the time point advances through the eight developmental time points (e10.5, e11.5, e12.5, e13.5, e14.5, e15.5, e16.5, and P0). Not all tissues are available at all time points.
