Description

This track shows the draft assembly (Dec. 2009, Sanger Institute/WormBase WS210) of the $organism genome.

In dense mode, this track depicts the contigs. Contig boundaries are distinguished by the use of alternating gold and brown coloration.

All components within this track are of fragment type "D": Whole Genome Shotgun draft contig. The mitochondrial sequence, genbank accession NC_010383.2, is of type "F": finished sequence.

Credits

The Dec. 2009 Haemonchus contortus (Barber Pole worm) draft assembly (Sanger Institute,WormBase WS210) was produced by the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute sequencing center.
