Description

This track shows locations of likely contamination in the assembly, i.e. high-scoring alignments of E.coli, vector and maize mitochondrion sequence. The name for each feature indicates the type of contamination found there.

The maize mitochondrion contamination ("MAIZE_MITO") locations are for the entire WGS contig found to be contaminated. The E.coli and vector locations show only the contaminated portion of the affected WGS contig.

Thanks to NCBI and Washington University, St. Louis for providing these data.
