UCSC Genome Browser: Site Down for Emergency Maintenance

This UCSC Genome Browser web server is currently down for emergency maintenance. Our system administrators are working to bring the site back as soon as possible. Please check our Bluesky feed for updates and downtime estimates.

In the meantime, please use one of the UCSC Genome Browser alternate servers. Please note that saved sessions and custom tracks are not copied between these mirrors:

  • U.S.: genome.ucsc.edu
  • Europe mirror: genome-euro.ucsc.edu
  • Asia mirror: genome-asia.ucsc.edu

Help & Support

To reach the UCSC Genome Browser support team, please email our confidential email list (not publicly displayed) at: genome-www@soe.ucsc.edu. In addition, the UCSC Genome Browser public support forum may contain details about this outage.
