Description

This track displays probe information for the chicken 13k microarray (see reference). There are 13,174 probes in this set.

References

Burnside J, Neiman P, Tang J, Basom R, Talbot R, Aronszajn M, Burt D, Delrow J. Development of a cDNA array for chicken gene expression analysis. BMC Genomics. 2005 Feb 4;6(1):13. [PubMed]
